About Us

Savitri Jyoti Foundation is a registered non-profit based in Pune, dedicated to transforming lives in slum communities through education and women empowerment.

We help children get access to school, provide identity documents like Aadhaar cards, and support women through skill training and self-help groups. In 2024 alone, we helped over 40+ children join school and supported 100+ women in building independent livelihoods.

  • Enrolled underserved children in local schools
  • Helped families get Aadhaar and legal documents
  • Ran classes and workshops for school readiness
  • Trained women in income-generating skills

We believe real change comes from the ground up — and every donation, every volunteer, and every shared message helps us go further.
